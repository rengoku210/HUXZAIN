13. CHAT MONITORING & ANTI-FRAUD SYSTEM

One of the most important systems still missing is chat monitoring and fraud prevention.
Since HUXZAIN is a marketplace, many users will try to move transactions outside the platform to avoid fees or potentially scam each other.
We want a complete protection system.
CHAT WARNING SYSTEM
Whenever a buyer and seller open chat, a warning banner should appear.
Example warnings:
• Do not share phone numbers.
• Do not share personal email addresses.
• Do not complete payments outside HUXZAIN.
• Transactions outside HUXZAIN are not protected.
• HUXZAIN cannot help recover losses from off-platform deals.
These warnings should always remain visible.
AUTOMATIC DETECTION SYSTEM
The system should automatically scan messages.
Examples:
Phone Numbers
WhatsApp Numbers
Telegram Usernames
Discord IDs
Email Addresses
Instagram Usernames
External Website Links
UPI IDs
Bank Details
Whenever these are detected:
System should create a warning.
System should store the event.
System should increase risk score.
CHAT RISK LEVELS
Low Risk
Medium Risk
High Risk
Critical Risk
Example:
One phone number = medium risk.
Repeated attempts = high risk.
Repeated external payment discussion = critical risk.
ADMIN CHAT REVIEW
We need a section where staff can review flagged chats.
Show:
• Buyer
• Seller
• Order
• Flag reason
• Risk level
• Date
When clicked:
Open complete conversation.
Highlight suspicious messages automatically.
This will save huge amounts of review time.
FRAUD ALERTS
If users repeatedly try sharing external contact details:
Admin should receive alerts.
Moderator should receive alerts.
Risk score should increase automatically.
This helps us identify problematic users early.
